Our Story

FUEL THE PRIDE

Throughout my childhood, art and sports took up most of my time. My mother kept me busy by signing me up for any sport the YMCA's bulletin board offered. When I wasn't active outdoors, my sketch book was open, and I was doodling anything that came to my imagination. These two activities would eventually converge and become the Hemero brand.

My opportunity came in 2013, my freshman year of college, when my former teammates from high school asked me to design jackets for the school basketball team. I realized this was the chance to merge my passion for sports and design, and I seized it without hesitation. Shortly after the semester ended, I packed my bags and booked a one-way ticket to Vietnam to pursue my passion.

At the time I had no prior experience in manufacturing; therefore, I faced some challenges. Luckily, my uncle, Minh, ran a denim manufacturer and was the ideal person to mentor me. With his experience and resources, we were ready to make a quality product in no time.
