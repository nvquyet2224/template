AFFILIATE PROGRAM

Hemero is a sportswear brand specializing in ready-to-wear and custom basketball apparel. We design and produce most of our products in-house to provide our customers with exceptional quality at an affordable price. The Hemero affiliate program is a great way for coaches, trainers, athletic directors, athletes and the like to earn a commission through teamwear and retail orders.

How Does It Work?

Hemero will provide you with a unique link that would connect your followers to Hemero’s online store. Additionally, we will also provide you with creative assets that you could place on your website and/or social media profiles. We will pay you a commission rate of 10% of all sales that you refer to our site on a monthly basis.

How are orders tracked?

When a customer clicks on your unique link, your affiliate ID is stored in a cookie within that customer’s browser. Since our program works on a 60 day cookie, you will receive credit for teamwear orders and a selection of retail products within that time frame.
